MANDAR EDUCATION SOCIETY

Konkan is a most picturesque yet most backward region of coastal Maharashtra.The youth of Konkan region was deprived of technical education for years. It was Mr. Rajaram Shinde's concern and vision to enable this youth with some kind of technical education.He bravely ventured and started an institute in 1983,which is today catering to about 2500 students,giving technical education. Mandar Education Society campus includes the institutes of Management,Engineering, Polytechnic, B.Ed,Pharmacy and many more.The MES Institute always leading towards the Technology.
